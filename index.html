Ethnic Filter is a data visualization which shows the lack of diversity in design through webcam blur filters. Click anywhere to move through the ethnicities and filters. The data is from AIGA and Google's 2016 Design Census. In all there were 7205 Whites, 998 Hispanics, 951 Asians and 349 Blacks. White was set to 0 while the others were mapped to a range from 10 to 35 using linear scaling. Pro tip: squint your eyes for equality. Commissioned by AIGA for Design Census Gallery.
